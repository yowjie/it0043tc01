Sonic the Hedgehog

The Quickster

Sonic the Hedgehog (ソニック・ザ・ヘッジホッグ) is the title character, and he is also the main character of the Sonic the Hedgehog series.

He is an alien anthropomorphic dark blue hedgehog gifted with the ability to run at the speed of sound and beyond, hence to his name, and possesses lightning fast reflexes to match.

Knuckles the Echidna

Spiky-Headed

Knuckles the Echidna (ナックルズ・ザ・エキドゥナ, Nakkurusu za Ekiduna) is one of the main characters in the anime series Sonic X.

Knuckles is a character from Sega's Sonic the Hedgehog series. He is a red anthropomorphic short-beaked echidna who is Sonic's secondary best friend and former rival. Determined and serious, but sometimes gullible, he fights his enemies using brute force and strength.

Doctor Eggman

The Overlander

Eggman (ドクター・エッグマン) formerly known as Dr. Ivo Robotnik (ドクター・ロボトニック) is the main villain of the Sonic the Hedgehog series. He is a Overlander and is the main arch nemesis of Sonic the Hedgehog.

Dr. Eggman is Sonic's eternal arch-enemy and nemesis, whom he has known longer than anyone else. Along with Tails, Eggman is one of the figures who has often shaped Sonic's destiny.

Miles Prower

Fox Boy

Miles Prower (マイルス・パウアー) also known as Tails (Japanese: テイルス) is a two-tailed fox with unbelievable intelligence. He is Sonic's best friend and sidekick.

Miles is a talented master engineer, when Tails isn't hanging out with his friends or saving the world with Sonic, he's usually in his workshop tinkering with mechanics. His smarts and helpful nature come in handy in a pinch, or when Sonic needs a voice of reason to keep his head straight.

Shadow the Hedgehog

The Blackwind

Shadow the Hedgehog (シャドウ・ザ・ヘッジホッグ) is a character that appears in the Sonic the Hedgehog series.

Shadow is Sonic's arch-rival. They look almost identical (to the extent that many initially got them confused), and they can match each other in speed, agility and abilities including Chaos Control. The two of them first met each other as enemies.
